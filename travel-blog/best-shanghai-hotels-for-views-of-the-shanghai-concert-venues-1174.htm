Best Shanghai Hotels for Views of the Shanghai Concert Venues

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Shanghai is a city that never sleeps, blending modernity with tradition in a way that captivates every traveler. For music lovers, catching a concert at one of Shanghai’s iconic venues is a must-do experience. But why not elevate your trip by staying at a hotel that offers breathtaking views of these concert halls? Whether you're attending a show at the Mercedes-Benz Arena, the Shanghai Grand Theatre, or the Bandai Namco Shanghai Base, these hotels will ensure your stay is as memorable as the performances.

Luxury Stays with Unbeatable Skyline Views

The Ritz-Carlton Shanghai, Pudong

Perched high above the Lujiazui skyline, The Ritz-Carlton Shanghai, Pudong offers panoramic views of the Huangpu River and the Mercedes-Benz Arena. The hotel’s floor-to-ceiling windows in its suites provide an unobstructed vista of the city’s glittering lights, making it a perfect spot for pre- or post-concert relaxation. The Flair Rooftop Bar is an added bonus, where you can sip cocktails while gazing at the arena’s futuristic architecture.

Waldorf Astoria Shanghai on the Bund

For those who prefer classic elegance, the Waldorf Astoria Shanghai on the Bund combines historic charm with modern luxury. Many rooms overlook the Bund and, on clear nights, you can even spot the Shanghai Grand Theatre in the distance. The hotel’s Long Bar is legendary, offering a sophisticated atmosphere to unwind after a night of live music.

Mid-Range Gems Close to the Action

Les Suites Orient, Bund Shanghai

Located just a short walk from the Bandai Namco Shanghai Base, Les Suites Orient offers stylish rooms with floor-to-ceiling windows showcasing the Huangpu River and Pudong’s skyline. The rooftop pool is a standout feature, providing a serene escape with a view of the concert venues below.

The Middle House

Nestled in the trendy Jing’an District, The Middle House is a short taxi ride away from multiple concert venues. The minimalist design and spacious rooms make it a favorite among travelers who appreciate understated luxury. Request a high-floor room for a stunning nighttime view of Shanghai’s illuminated landmarks.

Budget-Friendly Options with Great Views

Radisson Blu Hotel Shanghai New World

If you’re looking for affordability without sacrificing the view, the Radisson Blu Shanghai New World delivers. Located near People’s Square, it’s within walking distance of the Shanghai Grand Theatre. The hotel’s revolving restaurant offers 360-degree views, perfect for spotting concert venues while enjoying a meal.

Campanile Shanghai Bund Hotel

For budget-conscious travelers, Campanile Shanghai Bund Hotel provides clean, comfortable rooms with glimpses of the Bund and Pudong. While the views aren’t as expansive as luxury hotels, the proximity to venues like the Mercedes-Benz Arena makes it a practical choice.

Unique Stays for Music Enthusiasts

The Waterhouse at South Bund

This boutique hotel blends industrial chic with cozy comfort, offering rooms with private balconies overlooking the Huangpu River. It’s a favorite among creatives and music lovers, with its proximity to smaller indie venues like Yuyintang.

Swatch Art Peace Hotel

More than just a hotel, Swatch Art Peace Hotel is an artistic hub. Many rooms feature quirky designs and views of the Bund, making it ideal for those who want a culturally rich stay. The nearby Shanghai Concert Hall is just a short stroll away.

Tips for Booking the Perfect View

  • Check room descriptions carefully: Not all rooms in these hotels offer concert venue views, so confirm before booking.
  • Time your stay: Concerts often sell out fast, so book your hotel early to secure the best rooms.
  • Explore nearby dining: Many of these hotels are close to top restaurants, perfect for a pre-show dinner.

Shanghai’s concert venues are as dazzling as the performances they host, and staying at a hotel with a view ensures your trip is nothing short of spectacular. Whether you’re splurging on luxury or sticking to a budget, these options will keep you close to the music and the magic of Shanghai.
