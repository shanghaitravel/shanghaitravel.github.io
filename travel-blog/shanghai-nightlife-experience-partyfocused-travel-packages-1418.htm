Shanghai Nightlife Experience: Party-Focused Travel Packages

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Shanghai is a city that never sleeps, and its nightlife scene is legendary. From rooftop bars with skyline views to underground clubs pulsing with electronic beats, the city offers an unforgettable party experience. For travelers looking to dive into Shanghai’s vibrant after-dark culture, party-focused travel packages are the perfect way to experience the best of what the city has to offer.

Why Choose a Party-Focused Travel Package?

Navigating Shanghai’s nightlife can be overwhelming, especially for first-time visitors. A curated travel package takes the guesswork out of planning, ensuring you hit the hottest spots without missing a beat. These packages often include:

  • VIP access to top clubs and lounges
  • Exclusive drink deals or open-bar options
  • Guided pub crawls with like-minded travelers
  • Rooftop bar-hopping tours for stunning city views
  • Late-night food stops to refuel after dancing

The Best Nightlife Districts in Shanghai

Shanghai’s nightlife is spread across several key districts, each with its own vibe. Here’s where the magic happens:

The Bund: Glamour and Skyline Views

The Bund is home to some of Shanghai’s most luxurious bars and clubs. Think champagne lounges with panoramic views of Pudong’s neon skyline.

Xintiandi: Sophisticated Cocktails and Live Music

This upscale area blends historic Shikumen architecture with modern nightlife. Perfect for those who enjoy jazz bars or craft cocktails.

Found 158: The Underground Party Hub

A hidden gem for electronic music lovers, Found 158 is a subterranean complex packed with clubs like Arkham and Elevator.

Jing’an: Trendy Bars and Rooftop Vibes

Jing’an is where expats and locals mingle. Don’t miss The Cannery or Flask, a speakeasy-style bar behind a vending machine.

What to Expect in a Shanghai Party Package

Nightclub VIP Experience

Skip the lines and enjoy bottle service at top venues like Myst or Taxx, where international DJs often headline.

Craft Cocktail Tours

Shanghai’s mixology scene is world-class. A guided tour might include stops at Speak Low (ranked among Asia’s best bars) or Sober Company.

Late-Night Street Food Adventures

After hours, head to Sinan Mansions or a local xiaolongbao spot to recharge before the next round.

Themed Party Nights

From masquerade balls to neon raves, Shanghai’s clubs frequently host themed events—check if your package includes one!

Pro Tips for Maximizing Your Shanghai Night Out

  • Dress to impress – Many high-end clubs enforce a dress code.
  • Carry cash – Some smaller bars don’t accept cards.
  • Download DiDi – Shanghai’s ride-hailing app is a lifesaver after midnight.
  • Stay safe – Stick with your group and keep an eye on your belongings.

Shanghai’s nightlife is an adventure waiting to be explored. With a well-planned party package, you’ll experience the city’s electric energy without the hassle—just pure, unforgettable fun.
