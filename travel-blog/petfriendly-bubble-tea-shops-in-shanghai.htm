Pet-Friendly Bubble Tea Shops in Shanghai

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Shanghai is a city that never sleeps, and neither do its pet lovers. With the rise of pet-friendly spaces, bubble tea shops have joined the trend, offering a sweet escape for both humans and their furry companions. Whether you’re a local or a traveler exploring the city with your four-legged friend, these pet-friendly bubble tea spots are a must-visit. Here’s your ultimate guide to the best places where you can sip boba and spoil your pup at the same time.

Why Pet-Friendly Bubble Tea Shops Are a Big Deal

In a bustling metropolis like Shanghai, finding spaces where pets are welcome can be a challenge. Cafés and restaurants often have strict no-pet policies, but the bubble tea scene is breaking the mold. These shops aren’t just serving up delicious drinks—they’re creating a community for pet owners to relax, socialize, and enjoy quality time with their animals.

The Rise of Pet Culture in Shanghai

Shanghai’s pet culture has exploded in recent years, with more households adopting dogs, cats, and even exotic pets. The demand for pet-friendly venues has grown, and bubble tea shops are stepping up. From Instagram-worthy décor to special pet menus, these spots are redefining what it means to be a pet-inclusive business.

Top Pet-Friendly Bubble Tea Shops in Shanghai

1. Paws & Boba

Located in the heart of the French Concession, Paws & Boba is a haven for pet owners. The shop features a spacious outdoor seating area where dogs can roam freely while their owners enjoy signature drinks like the "Puppuccino Pearl Milk Tea."

Highlights:
- A dedicated pet play zone with toys and water stations.
- "Barkuterie" boards (pet-friendly snacks) for furry guests.
- Weekly "Yappy Hour" events with discounts for pet owners.

2. Wagging Tail Tea House

This cozy spot in Jing’an District is perfect for those who want to unwind with their pets. The menu includes organic, pet-safe bubble teas (yes, really!) made with lactose-free milk and natural sweeteners.

Must-Try:
- The "Wagging Tail Special" (a peanut butter-flavored boba for dogs).
- Matcha latte with oat milk for humans.

3. Bubble Paws Café

If you’re near Xintiandi, Bubble Paws Café is a must-visit. Known for its photogenic interiors and pet-friendly staff, this shop even hosts adoption events in collaboration with local shelters.

Pro Tip:
- Try their "Meowcha" series—cat-themed drinks with adorable paw-print toppings.

What Makes These Shops Stand Out?

Pet-Centric Amenities

From water bowls to pet-friendly seating, these shops go the extra mile to ensure comfort for both pets and owners. Some even offer leash hooks and pet-waste stations.

Social Media Appeal

Let’s be honest—part of the fun is capturing the perfect shot of your pet with a colorful boba drink. These shops are designed with Instagram in mind, featuring playful backdrops and pet-sized props.

Community Events

Many of these spots host pet meetups, themed parties, and even pet costume contests. It’s a great way to meet fellow animal lovers while enjoying a refreshing drink.

Tips for Visiting Pet-Friendly Bubble Tea Shops

  1. Check Policies in Advance
    Some shops may have size restrictions or require pets to be leashed. A quick call or social media check can save you time.

  2. Bring Pet Essentials
    While many shops provide water and treats, it’s always good to have your pet’s favorite toy or blanket on hand.

  3. Respect Other Guests
    Not everyone is a pet lover, so keep your furry friend well-behaved and avoid overcrowded times if your pet gets anxious.

Final Sips

Shanghai’s pet-friendly bubble tea scene is more than just a trend—it’s a reflection of the city’s evolving love for pets and innovative dining experiences. Whether you’re a local or just passing through, these spots offer a unique way to enjoy the city’s vibrant culture with your best friend by your side. So grab a leash, order a boba, and let the good times roll!
