Best Hotels in Shanghai for Large Groups

Home / Travel Blog / Blog Details

Shanghai, a dazzling metropolis where East meets West, is a top destination for travelers worldwide. Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, a family reunion, or a friends' getaway, finding the right hotel for a large group can be challenging. To make your trip seamless, we’ve curated a list of the best hotels in Shanghai that cater to big groups, offering spacious accommodations, top-notch amenities, and prime locations.

Luxury Stays for Large Groups

The Ritz-Carlton Shanghai, Pudong

Located in the heart of Lujiazui, The Ritz-Carlton Shanghai, Pudong is a dream destination for large groups seeking luxury. The hotel features expansive suites and interconnected rooms, perfect for families or corporate teams.

Why It’s Great for Groups:
- Spacious Suites: Many rooms offer stunning views of the Huangpu River and The Bund.
- Exclusive Meeting Spaces: Ideal for business travelers with private conference rooms.
- Dining Options: Multiple restaurants serving international cuisines to satisfy diverse tastes.

Waldorf Astoria Shanghai on The Bund

Steeped in history and elegance, the Waldorf Astoria is a favorite among large groups who appreciate classic luxury. The hotel’s heritage wing offers grand suites with vintage charm, while the modern tower provides contemporary comfort.

Group Perks:
- Historic Grand Ballroom: Perfect for weddings or gala events.
- Butler Service: Ensures personalized attention for every guest.
- Central Location: Steps away from The Bund and Nanjing Road.

Budget-Friendly Options for Big Groups

Radisson Blu Hotel Shanghai New World

For travelers looking for affordability without sacrificing comfort, Radisson Blu is an excellent choice. The hotel offers large rooms and easy access to popular attractions like People’s Square and Yu Garden.

Group Benefits:
- Flexible Room Configurations: Twin and triple rooms available.
- On-Site Dining: A buffet restaurant with diverse meal options.
- Public Transport Hub: Convenient for group excursions.

Holiday Inn Shanghai Pudong Kangqiao

Situated in the rapidly developing Pudong district, this Holiday Inn is perfect for large groups on a budget. The hotel provides family-friendly amenities and easy access to Shanghai Disneyland.

Why Groups Love It:
- Affordable Rates: Great value for money.
- Shuttle Services: Free shuttles to key attractions.
- Kid-Friendly Facilities: Play areas and family rooms available.

Unique Boutique Hotels for Group Stays

The Waterhouse at South Bund

This trendy boutique hotel combines industrial chic with modern comfort, making it a hit with creative groups and young travelers. The rooftop bar offers panoramic views of the Huangpu River.

Standout Features:
- Loft-Style Rooms: Spacious and stylish for group gatherings.
- Artistic Vibe: A favorite among design enthusiasts.
- Social Spaces: Communal areas encourage group interaction.

URBN Hotel Shanghai

China’s first carbon-neutral hotel, URBN is perfect for eco-conscious groups. The intimate setting and personalized service make it ideal for smaller large groups (10-15 people).

Eco-Friendly Highlights:
- Sustainable Design: Recycled materials used throughout.
- Private Courtyard: Great for group events.
- Local Experiences: Customized tours of Shanghai’s hidden gems.

Serviced Apartments for Extended Group Stays

Ascott Huaihai Road Shanghai

For groups planning longer stays, serviced apartments like Ascott provide the comforts of home. The fully equipped kitchens and living spaces are perfect for families or business teams.

Extended Stay Advantages:
- Apartment-Style Living: Separate bedrooms and common areas.
- Laundry Facilities: Convenient for long-term guests.
- Prime Shopping District: Close to high-end boutiques and cafes.

Fraser Suites Shanghai

Located in the bustling Jing’an district, Fraser Suites offers spacious two- and three-bedroom units. The rooftop pool and fitness center add extra appeal for groups.

Why It Works for Groups:
- Multiple Bedrooms: Ideal for families or colleagues.
- 24/7 Concierge: Helps with bookings and recommendations.
- Walking Distance to Nightlife: Great for groups who love to explore.

Tips for Booking Hotels for Large Groups in Shanghai

  1. Book Early – Popular hotels fill up fast, especially during peak seasons like Chinese New Year or Golden Week.
  2. Negotiate Group Rates – Many hotels offer discounts for bulk bookings.
  3. Check Room Configurations – Ensure the hotel can accommodate your group’s needs (interconnected rooms, extra beds, etc.).
  4. Consider Location – Proximity to public transport or attractions can save time and hassle.
  5. Read Reviews – Look for feedback from other large groups to gauge suitability.

Shanghai’s hospitality scene has something for every type of group traveler. Whether you’re splurging on a five-star experience or keeping it budget-friendly, these hotels ensure a memorable stay in one of the world’s most dynamic cities.
